Labbayk allahumma Umratan

Talbiyah: Labbayka llahumma labbayk, Labbayka la sharika laka labbayk, innal hamda wa ni’mata, laka wal mulk, la sharika lak. 

At Hajr Al-Aswad: Bimillahi Wallahu Akbar

Between the Yamani corner and the Hajr Al-Aswad: Rabbana Atina Fid Dunya Hasanah, wa fil akhirati hasanah, wa qina Azab annar.

Innas safa wal marwata min sha e rillah.
